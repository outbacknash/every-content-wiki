# Loops vs Prompts

A paradigm shift in how knowledge workers interact with AI models like [[entities/anthropic-fable-5]].

## Definition
Loops vs Prompts is a mental model that contrasts linear, single-turn AI interactions ("Prompts") with iterative, asynchronous, and autonomous workflows ("Loops") where the AI handles full projects.

## How It Works
A "gardening" approach to work where the user:
1. Delegates a project or a sequence of tasks.
2. Allows the agent to work asynchronously.
3. Reviews the intermediate or final outputs.
4. Feeds learnings back into the next "run" or loop.

## Business Impact
As models become more capable of long-running, autonomous work, the human role shifts from *writer* to *editor* or *director*. This allows for a massive increase in output (e.g., Kieran Klaassen clearing backlogs in hours) but requires higher-level delegation skills.
