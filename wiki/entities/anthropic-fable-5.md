# Anthropic Fable 5

Claude Fable 5 is Anthropic's state-of-the-art AI model released in June 2026.

## Overview
Widely regarded as the "best coding model in the world," Fable 5 represents a significant leap in delegable AI capability. It is characterized by its high reasoning ability, although it is also noted for being "token-hungry" and more expensive than predecessor models.

## Key Products/Contributions
- **Superior Coding:** Highly effective for bug fixes, feature requests, and complex technical tasks.
- **Project Delegation:** Designed for Level 7/8 AI adoption where users delegate whole projects rather than single prompts.
- **Resource Intensive:** Costs roughly twice as much as standard models and has high token consumption.
- **"Mythos-level" Capability:** Described by the Every team as a model that demands a new way of approaching knowledge work.
