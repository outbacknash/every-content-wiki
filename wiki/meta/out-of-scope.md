# Out of Scope Topics

The following topics are excluded from the Every Content Wiki to maintain focus on AI workflows and Every.to content:

1. **Generic AI News**: General industry news that doesn't impact Every's specific workflows.
2. **Personal Data**: Specific private details about individuals not relevant to their professional roles.
3. **Unrelated Technical Documentation**: Infrastructure or code docs not directly tied to content production or AI tool usage.
