# The Moral of Fable

## Key Points
- [[entities/anthropic-fable-5]] is the "best coding model in the world."
- Success with Fable 5 depends on the user's level of AI adoption—specifically reaching Level 7 or 8.
- The core transition is from "Prompts" to "Loops."
- Developer-centric "Loop" workflows are beginning to spread to general knowledge work (e.g., slide decks via Cowork).
- Knowledge work is increasingly resembling "gardening."
