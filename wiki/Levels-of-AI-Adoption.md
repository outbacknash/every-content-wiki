# Levels of AI Adoption
Type: Concept | Category: Framework

A framework developed by Mike Taylor (building on Steve Yegge's work) to map the stages of AI integration in personal and professional workflows.

## The 8 Levels
- **Level 1:** Basic Chatbot (simple Q&A).
- **Level 7/8:** Orchestrator/Delegator. Users delegate entire projects, allow agents to work asynchronously, review results, and feed learnings back into the next iteration.

## Core Principle
A higher level is not inherently "better." The appropriate level depends on:
1. **Trust:** How much you trust the AI to run without intervention.
2. **Cost of Error:** How damaging a mistake would be for the specific task.

## Key Transitions
Moving from "Prompts" (single-turn instructions) to "Loops" (iterative, multi-step autonomous processes) is the primary signal of reaching the upper levels of adoption.
