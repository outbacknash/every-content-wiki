# Loops vs Prompts
Type: Concept | Category: AI Workflow

A paradigm shift in how knowledge workers interact with AI models like [[Anthropic Fable 5]].

## Prompts
Traditional interaction involving single-turn, linear instructions. This is the "old" way of using AI, typically corresponding to lower [[Levels of AI Adoption]].

## Loops
A "gardening" approach to work where the user:
1. Delegates a project or a sequence of tasks.
2. Allows the agent to work asynchronously.
3. Reviews the intermediate or final outputs.
4. Feeds learnings back into the next "run" or loop.

## Importance
As models become more capable of long-running, autonomous work, the human role shifts from *writer* to *editor* or *director*.
