# Bitcoin Dataset

## Definition
The Bitcoin dataset comprises the entire history of the Bitcoin blockchain, including all transactions, blocks, and addresses since the genesis block in 2009. This immutable ledger provides a transparent record of all peer-to-peer electronic cash transfers within the Bitcoin network.

## Core Principles
- Decentralization: The ledger is maintained by a global network of nodes without a central authority.
- Immutability: Once a transaction is confirmed in a block, it is computationally impractical to alter or remove.
- Transparency: Every transaction is publicly verifiable, ensuring the integrity of the total supply and transfer history.
